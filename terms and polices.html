Privacy Policies

1. Data Collection

We collect the following personal information when you sign up for SyncApp:

  • Name
  • Email

2. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used for:

  • User identification
  • Personalization of your experience in SyncApp

3. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you have an active account with SyncApp.

4. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account or personal data, please contact us at smturbo23@gmail.com.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. All data is stored within SyncApp (Firebase).

5.1. Image Sharing

Images uploaded through SyncApp are stored securely using third-party hosting services (e.g., imgbb). We do not process or access your images, but images may be accessible via a direct URL. Users are strongly advised not to upload personal, sensitive, or confidential information such as ID cards, passwords, or addresses.

5.2. Emoji Usage

SyncApp includes an emoji keyboard for expressive communication. Emojis are rendered using open-source fonts and libraries (e.g., Twemoji). We do not track individual emoji usage, and no personal data is collected through this feature.

6. Tracking Technologies

We use tracking to send notifications to users.

7. Age Restriction

SyncApp is available to all ages.

8. Policy Updates

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in the changelog.

9. Contact Us
